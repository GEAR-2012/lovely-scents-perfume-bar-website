The hand decanted perfume

Our decanted perfume product is mostly made from French ingredients produced by our supplier. Its composition is the same in all ‘Eau De Toilets’ quality, with all its physical and chemical characteristics. We can keep a low commercial price because of the scents not sold in the original packaging (genuine perfume bottle/box) but refill perfume bottles (10, 11, 30, 50, 100ml) and our supplier produces them in Hungary from the ingredients. Our list ranges from classic to the latest scents perfume types. In our store, on wooden shelving system we decant each different scent into glass bottles by hand, from 1 litre glass containers with copper taps. Our clients can choose from the perfume list of scents which they prefer, from the testers on the counter they can also try it and we can fill it in the desired sized bottle. Later the empty bottle can be refilled at an even better price with the same or a different fragrance.

packaging

The various fragrances are sold from 1 litre decorative glass containers with a copper tap, each with a fragrance tester in the form of glass bottles. The fragrance samples can be examined by the clients, thus making their choice easier. Also, a comprehensive list of fragrances - both classical and up-to-date - will help you make your decision. Our shop offers 64 kinds of fragrances for all ages. We also provide perfume dispensers of various sizes and shapes. You can find in our shop packaging materials as well such as paper bags, baskets etc.. We are looking forward to welcoming all our existing and prospective customers to our shop.
